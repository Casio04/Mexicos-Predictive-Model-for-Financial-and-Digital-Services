Machine Learning Model

Description

Having in mind the information and patterns we saw in the previous dashboards, the next step was training a model with all the information available and considering the relationships we identified among every gathered variable in the digital and financial environment up to 2020.
The fundamental question that we answer with a machine learning model is:
Which is the predicted effect of financial and digital variables based on changes in other variables?
For instance, which is the effect of an increase in computer consumption in a municipality over streaming subscriptions?
