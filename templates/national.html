MEXICO'S NATIONAL SITUATION

Discovering patterns

Before we access to a detailed situation on each state, we need to look at the current situation nationwide. On this first map, you can see six of the most important financial services through the country and their distribution. With just slight changes, there are some patterns we can spot immediately.
We can see that Mexico City, the State of Mexico (or just Mexico in the map), Nuevo Leon and sometimes Aguascalientes, are the states where these services have reached more people so far.
Moreover, if we are curious enough to see the colored detail for each service, we can notice how Mobile Banking Contracts (MB on the map list) have less coverage on those same states, indicating a valuable opportunity there.
But... what services are linked to mobile banking so you can increase that so-needed availabilty? We will discover it in just a few minutes.

Back to basics

Having in mind those states with better or worse access for different services, here we use the old-fashioned scatter plot to understand how variables could be possibly related to each other.
For instance, if we pick mobile banking on any axis and start comparing it with any other service or product, we can see how related they are (or not) between them.
Choosing cellphone, for example, would tell us there is a direct relationship between both (and it should be), however, we also can see Mexico City has a different behavior than every other state.
This pecularities sometimes are the most important ones to take a decision and, of course, we will explore them.

So... Is a million too much?

If you are totally dedicated to sell or promote one or more of the displayed services, you probably know these numbers in detail and know exactly what they represent, but what if you are just starting from scracth, you are just curious or simply it's a brand new market for you?
Here, we used demographic data to know how many services or products are availble for each 10,000 people, so you can find out if it is a saturated market or if it is one you should look into.
Moreover, you can also hover on each state to see if that particular region is on average or not. In that way, you can start thinking of a place where your services could be needed, and there is where we are going.
